What it does

A playful reading workflow from idea to story.

1

Start with a prompt

Type or record a story idea, then choose the child's age, theme, lesson, tone, and story length.

2

Generate a full book

Story Sprout creates a title, pages, illustrations, and optional narration in the selected language.

3

Read and share

Open the book in a full-screen reader, listen together, rate favorites, and share a link when you want.

App screenshots

Designed for parents, inviting for children.

For parents

Creative controls without a complicated setup.

Parents can guide each story by age group, language, theme, tone, and learning goal. Books can stay private or be published to the public library when the author chooses to share them.

  • Multiple story languages for multilingual families.
  • Voice input for children who would rather talk than type.
  • Optional photo-to-character input for personalized stories.
  • Public reports and moderation controls for shared books.

Privacy-minded by design

Uploaded character photos are used only to create a storybook-style character description. The app does not keep the original photo as part of the generated book.
