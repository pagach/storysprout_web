Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Story Sprout handles information when families use the app to create, read, and share AI-generated picture books. Story Sprout is designed for parents and guardians to use with children.

Information we collect

Story Sprout may process the following information:

  • Story prompts, selected language, age range, theme, lesson, tone, and story length.
  • Generated book content, illustrations, narration files, ratings, reports, and public sharing status.
  • An app installation identifier used to manage sessions, free generations, push notifications, and account state.
  • Optional email address if a parent provides it for account or support purposes.
  • Payment-related purchase tokens when in-app purchases are verified.
  • Analytics, crash diagnostics, device information, and notification tokens through Firebase services.

Photo-to-character input

If you upload or take a photo to create a personalized character, the photo is used to create a child-safe, storybook-style character description. The original photo is not stored as part of the generated book. Do not upload photos unless you have the right to use them and are comfortable using them for story generation.

How we use information

  • To generate picture books, illustrations, narration, and book metadata.
  • To show public or private books in the app.
  • To send optional push notifications, such as when a book is ready.
  • To verify purchases and manage available generations.
  • To improve reliability, diagnose crashes, prevent abuse, and keep child-safe moderation controls working.

AI and service providers

Story Sprout uses AI service providers to generate story text, illustrations, narration, and optional character descriptions. Firebase may be used for analytics, crash reporting, and push notifications. Payment platforms such as Google Play may process purchases under their own policies.

Public books and sharing

Books are private unless they are published or shared. If a book is published to the public library, its title, description, generated pages, images, narration, language, ratings, and public metadata may be visible to other users. Private books can also be shared by link if the author chooses to create a share link.

Children's privacy

Story Sprout is intended to be used by parents or guardians with children. Children should not submit personal information without a parent or guardian. Parents should avoid entering sensitive personal details in prompts.

Data retention and deletion

Generated books and related media are kept so they can be displayed in the app. Operational logs and diagnostics may be kept for security, reliability, and abuse prevention. To request deletion or privacy support, use the support process provided through the app store listing.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the service, including encrypted transport, access controls, hashed identifiers where appropriate, and server-side validation. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Story Sprout changes. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated date.
